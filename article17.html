How to Start a Career in Tech: A Step-by-Step Guide

Posted on May 20, 2025 by Jamal Williams
Programming

The tech industry continues to offer exciting career opportunities in 2025. Whether you're a recent graduate or looking to switch careers, this guide will help you navigate your path into the tech world.

1. Education Paths

Traditional computer science degrees remain valuable, but alternative routes like coding bootcamps, online certifications, and self-directed learning have gained recognition. Choose based on your learning style and career goals.

2. Certifications to Consider

Cloud certifications (AWS, Azure, GCP), cybersecurity credentials (CISSP, CEH), and specialized programming certifications can boost your resume. Focus on ones relevant to your desired role.

3. Building a Portfolio

Create GitHub repositories with your code, contribute to open source projects, and develop personal projects that solve real problems. A strong portfolio often outweighs formal education in tech hiring.
