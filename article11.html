Best Laptops for Developers in 2025

Posted on November 15, 2025 by Sarah Johnson
Gadgets

Choosing the right laptop as a developer in 2025 requires balancing performance, portability, and specialized features. After testing dozens of models, here are our top picks for different development needs.

1. QuantumBook Pro

With its quantum co-processor and 64GB of unified memory, the QuantumBook Pro handles machine learning workloads and complex simulations with ease. The 18-hour battery life ensures productivity on the go.

2. DevStation X

This modular laptop lets you swap components like GPUs and storage drives. Perfect for developers who need different configurations for various projects. The 4K OLED display is ideal for design work.

3. CodeAir

At just 2.2 pounds with 14-hour battery life, the CodeAir is the ultimate portable coding machine. The tactile keyboard and 120Hz display make long coding sessions comfortable.
