Windows 13 Preview: What's New and Exciting

Posted on February 3, 2025 by Sarah Miller
Software

Microsoft has released the first preview build of Windows 13, and we've had exclusive early access to test its new features. This major update represents a significant shift in Microsoft's approach to desktop operating systems.

AI-Powered Interface

The most noticeable change is the AI-integrated desktop. Windows 13 learns your work patterns and automatically organizes files, suggests apps, and even anticipates your next actions. The new Copilot+ feature acts as a true digital assistant that can automate complex workflows.

Revamped Start Menu

The Start Menu has been completely redesigned with a dynamic layout that changes based on context. Working on a document? It surfaces relevant files and tools. Gaming? It shows your friends list and performance settings.

Enhanced Security

Windows 13 introduces quantum-resistant encryption and continuous authentication that uses facial recognition and typing patterns to verify your identity throughout your session.
